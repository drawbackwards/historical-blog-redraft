**Ladder by Drawbackwards is an AI-powered UX and CX insight tool that turns raw customer feedback into specific, actionable improvements -- in real time.** It integrates with your existing experience management platforms, scores your product against the Drawbackwards UX Success Ladder, and helps you make faster, better-informed decisions without drowning in data.

## What Is Ladder and What Does It Do?

Ladder is a customer feedback analysis tool built by Drawbackwards -- a UX and CX consultancy with decades of cross-industry experience. It uses artificial intelligence to cut through the noise of multi-source feedback and surface what actually matters.

If you're a Product Owner, Design Leader, Data Analyst, or Chief Experience Officer, you already know the problem: collecting CX and UX data is the easy part. Making sense of it is where things fall apart.

Ladder solves that. Here's what it does at its core:

- **AI-driven insights:** Analyzes customer feedback to highlight areas for improvement and suggest specific, actionable next steps
- **Real-time sentiment analysis:** Reads user-generated written feedback, assigns themes, and scores sentiment across your lines of business and products -- instantly
- **Seamless integration:** Connects with your existing experience management tools so there's no disruption to current workflows
- **Ladder scoring:** Pins your product's experience to the Drawbackwards UX Success Ladder so you always know where you stand and what to tackle next

## How Does Ladder Deliver Real-Time Customer Insights?

Ladder is built from real-world use cases -- the filters and features come from what Drawbackwards' own clients actually needed. That means they're tried and tested, and the tool keeps evolving as more people use it.

When you start with Ladder, there's a discovery and setup phase where the Drawbackwards team sits with you to understand your business. Then they tailor Ladder to your data sources and the specific types of insights that matter most to you. You get a custom version, not a generic dashboard.

From there, Ladder lets you:

- See how much activity occurred in each channel (chat complaints, third-party reviews, surveys)
- View your overall Ladder score for a specific workflow
- Filter data and generate a score for that filtered subset
- Compare filtered scores against your overall score to spot exactly where your product experience is rising or falling

## What Is Real-Time Sentiment Analysis and How Does Ladder Use It?

Real-time sentiment analysis is the ability to understand how customers feel -- right now, not in next quarter's report.

Ladder analyzes user-generated written feedback, identifies themes, and assigns sentiment scores continuously. You stay in tune with your customers' needs without waiting for someone to manually review a backlog of comments.

This matters because timing is everything. If a pain point is spiking in your checkout flow today, you need to know today -- not after it shows up in a monthly summary.
